Who registers third level titles?

National higher education institutions will be responsible for registering the degrees they issue, after verifying compliance with legal and academic requirements. The information registered will be part of the National System of Information of Higher Education and this will be the only official means through which the recognition and validity of the degree in Ecuador will be verified.

(General Regulations to the Organic Law of Higher Education, Art. 56).

Scope of title

The recognition/registration of the degree does not enable the exercise of professions regulated by specific laws, and especially the exercise of professions that directly put the life, health and citizen security in accordance with article 104 of the Organic Law of Higher Education.

Resolution RPC-SO-16-No.256-2016
